Black-Scholes Pricer

European option pricing from scratch — no scipy.stats, no QuantLib

Formula

d1 = [ln(S/K) + (r + σ²/2)T] / (σ√T)

d2 = d1 - σ√T

C = S·N(d1) - K·e-rT·N(d2)

P = K·e-rT·N(-d2) - S·N(-d1)

N(x) = 0.5·(1 + erf(x/√2))
